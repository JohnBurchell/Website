Self Driving Car University Project involving C++/C/Arduino

University Project

A project to tie in with our embedded systems course. This involved creating an autonomous vehicle with the aim of driving it around a track using image recognition to guide the vehicle. I Worked primarily on the software side of things. In particular I created a proxy to communicate between the low level board which controlled the servos etc and the higher level board which performed the calculations and plotted the course for the vehicle.

I was also responsible for creating a parking maneuver for the vehicle in a simulation and then in reality. Furthermore, I was responsible for the overall software design and I tried to help as much as possible where needed.

Unfortunately there isn't much more footage than what is found below but the project was a great success!
